Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, store, and protect your information.

Last updated: 7 July 2026 · Applies to neurofi.com and neurofi.in and the NeuroFi mobile application.

1. Who We Are

This Privacy Policy is issued by NeuroFi Wealth Advisory ("NeuroFi", "we", "us"), registered office at 33, 1st Floor, 1st Main, 1st Cross, Lakshmipura Layout, Devasandra, K R Puram, Bengaluru, Karnataka – 560036, India. This policy explains how we handle personal data of visitors to our website and users of our platform, in line with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 and applicable SEBI regulations governing Investment Advisers.

2. Information We Collect

  • Identity & contact data: name, email, phone number, and details you provide during onboarding or when you contact us.
  • Financial data: income, expenses, assets, liabilities, goals, and risk profile information you enter directly, and — where you give explicit consent — account and transaction data fetched via a SEBI-licensed Account Aggregator (such as OneMoney) for the purpose of financial analysis.
  • Payment data: subscription and transaction details processed through our payment gateway partner, PayU. NeuroFi does not store your card, UPI, or net-banking credentials — these are handled directly by PayU under its own security and compliance standards.
  • Communication data: messages, OTPs, and notifications sent to you via SMS or WhatsApp through our communications provider, Twilio.
  • Usage data: device information, log data, and app/website interaction data collected automatically to maintain and improve our services.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account and deliver the financial planning and analysis services you subscribe to.
  • To generate financial models, goal projections, and risk assessments — including through AI-assisted analysis, which is always reviewed by a human advisor before being shared with you.
  • To process payments and manage your subscription.
  • To send you account, service, and transactional communications (including OTPs).
  • To comply with applicable law, regulatory requests, and to detect and prevent fraud.

4. AI-Assisted Analysis

We use artificial intelligence models to help analyze your financial data and draft financial plans. Data shared with our AI systems is used strictly to generate your analysis and is not used to train third-party foundation models beyond what is necessary to provide our service. Every AI-generated output is reviewed by a human member of our advisory team before it is presented as guidance to you.

5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal or financial data. We share data only with:

  • Service providers who process data on our behalf under contractual confidentiality obligations — including our payment gateway (PayU), communications provider (Twilio), Account Aggregator partners (such as OneMoney, only with your explicit consent), and cloud/AI infrastructure providers.
  • Regulators and authorities where required by law, court order, or regulatory directive (including SEBI, once our registration is granted).
  • Successor entities in the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, subject to equivalent privacy protections.

6. Account Aggregator Data

Where you choose to link your bank or investment accounts via an Account Aggregator, data is fetched only after your explicit, purpose-specific consent through the Account Aggregator framework regulated by the Reserve Bank of India. You may withdraw this consent at any time through the Account Aggregator app you used to grant it, or by writing to us.

7. Data Security

We use industry-standard safeguards, including encryption in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest, access controls, and regular security reviews, to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal and financial data for as long as your account is active and for a reasonable period thereafter to comply with legal, accounting, and regulatory record-keeping obligations, or until you request deletion, whichever is later — subject to any statutory retention requirements that apply to financial services records.

9. Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to our legal and regulatory retention obligations.
  • Withdraw consent for Account Aggregator data access at any time.
  • Opt out of non-essential communications.

To exercise any of these rights, write to staff@neurofi.com.

10. Cookies

Our website may use essential cookies required for site functionality and analytics cookies to understand how visitors use our site. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be notified via email or an in-app notice. Continued use of our services after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions or requests, write to staff@neurofi.com or see our Contact Us page.
